How to play

Two players will take turns to place a token chosen by the opponent on the board. The first player who completes a horizontal, vertical or diagonal row of four pieces, all of which has at least one common attribute, wins.

There are altogether 16 game pieces, each piece has four attributes:

  • shape: round or square
  • ears: yes or no
  • borders: yes or no
  • animal type: rabbit or panda
